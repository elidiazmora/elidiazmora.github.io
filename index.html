About Me

Hello! I'm Elizabeth, a multidisciplinary Graphic Designer & Illustrator. I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Graphic Design & Printmaking. As a side project I founded puntogalgo.com, an online store of handmade accessories for pets. I find my inspiration in everyday life's things: patterns, books, artists like Agu Wu, Patryk Hardziej or Monica Sampietro, but especially my lovely dogs 🐾
